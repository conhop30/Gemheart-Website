Gemheart

Where Real Time Strategy meets cards.

Features

Economy Driven Interaction

With no economy resets between rounds, the option to directly attack your opponent's resources, and spend and save how you want, the game's pace is in your hands.

Fight For Information

Fog of War permeates each player's board, choking potentially devastating information. Push past it first to get the tactical advantage.

At Your Command

A spread of playstyles able to be combined enables your deckbuilding past the rigid rules of a single groups identity.
